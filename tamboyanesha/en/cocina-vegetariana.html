Culinary Vacations: Vegetarian Food

Tambo Yanesha invites couples, friends and fellow travelers to this vegetarian cooking retreat where you can prepare and eat Delicious meals, learn, explore, rest, improve your health and much more.

This program is designed for one or two people maximum for an intimate experience and personalized instruction and includes yoga and reiki sessions, although it is currently in the process of dissemination.

  • Description

    Vegetarian cuisine is both tasty and culturally diverse, this retreat It has the goal of showing just that. Come and discover new recipes, his talent as a cook, a new place, new people, as well as information that can inspire them to live a healthier and richer life. It is an intimate culinary escape; If you are vegetarian or vegan, you could expand your knowledge and learn new delicious recipes as observing possibilities of vegetarian dishes are endless, there are so many things we can do with fruits, vegetables, legumes (beans, lentils and peas), Whole grains, nuts and seeds are part of a balanced vegetarian diet. We will learn to transform these into delicious gastronomic experiences that will excite your palate and your senses. Our renowned chef is certified in vegetarian nutrition.
