TAMBO CONSERVATION

  • 21 Apr, 2019
  • 600 Views

To be able to Live in Harmony with Nature

The programs "Tambo Conservación" are designed to share with you our beloved hostel and the activities we do to be able to live in harmony with nature in a sustainable way.
We have a lagoon at your disposal for swimming and a path in the forest where you can appreciate the biodiversity of birds and plants.
Each bungalow is independent and has very comfortable beds with outdoor hammocks. The bathrooms are shared and separated from the tambos.

  • Description

    The "Tambo Conservación" programs include lodging, all meals (vegetarian), filtered water and the activities that will be coordinated with you.

    Detail program (in process), although we can advance this program includes three overnight stays, three dinners, three breakfasts, two lunches and two hikes.

    The transfer from Oxapampa (center) to Tambo Yanesha will be included and coordinated with a trusted driver for you.
