Help Others With their Self-Healing

Join the group for a journey of self-discovery as we explore This wonderful healing art and its incredible possibilities. Dedicate three days to discover a new capacity in itself.
"When he heals himself and helps others with his self-healing, he heals the Earth. You make a difference. "A quote from Laurel Steinhice, Channeling the Mother Earth (in Essential Reiki by D. Stein).
We invite you to participate in the Retreat of Reiki in Tambo Yanesha which consists of in tune with the first degree of Reiki and learn to heal through hands. Offer yourself the wonderful opportunity to learn and share the healing system Natural of the Master, which is a simple, non-invasive healing system that balances the energy centers of the body. It is one of the most holistic forms of healing easy and more natural that exists.

  • Description

    This is an effective workshop for people who are interested in stress management, self-healing and personal growth, with the added benefit of learning to channel Healing energy and helping others too. The ability to channel Reiki will remain you for your whole life and the more you work with him, the more you will be surprised at his power to inspire.

    Reiki is a Japanese technique to reduce stress and increase relaxation that promotes healing.

    It is a form of healing used to cleanse, balance and heal while invigorating mind, body and soul.

    For those of you who have never received a Reiki treatment, it is a very nice. During the treatment, you feel a deep relaxation and a great feeling of peace. A session usually lasts between 45 or 60 minutes, although it varies according to the needs of each person.

    Reiki is not taught in the usual sense. You can not learn from a book or self-taught but it is transferred by the Reiki Master to the student through the initiation, a simple ceremony that is repeated at intervals throughout the retreat.

    This 3-day / 2-night program is a wonderful "all-inclusive" option offered to each participant the opportunity to immerse themselves in the Reiki energy and just "be" in order to get to be more in touch with this energy so that it can flow more freely through you and his life. The guided yoga and meditation session, the delicious and healthy vegetarian meals and free time to enjoy the beautiful natural environment of this Conservation Area will help in his process of tuning in with Reiki.

    This program is for anyone who wants to be initiated into Reiki in a quiet environment and beautiful that allows you to connect with nature. It is possible to achieve your first level (of three) in a short stay in Tambo Yanesha. You will be able to channel Reiki to yourself and to the others before the end of this retreat.
